A **Background Record Review** is for candidates who want human judgment—not another checklist—before HR runs screening.

## What we look for

- Date and title mismatches across resume, LinkedIn, and forms
- Gaps that need consistent framing
- References who may be hard to reach or misaligned on titles
- Items you may need to disclose proactively

## What we don't do

We don't provide legal advice, run checks on your behalf, or contact employers. We prepare **you** to present a clear, consistent record.
