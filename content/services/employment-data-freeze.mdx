Commercial employment databases like **The Work Number** and **LexisNexis** hold payroll and screening data that lenders, landlords, and background check companies can pull on demand—often without you ever opting in. Freezing those records puts you back in control.

## What we help with

- **The Work Number (Equifax)** — employment and income verification data
- **LexisNexis** — screening and public-records databases used in background checks
- **Related services** — guidance on where else your data may live and how to restrict access

## What we don't do

We don't submit forms on your behalf without you present, provide legal advice, or guarantee a specific employer outcome. You complete each freeze in your own name—we make sure you have the right documents, the right fields, and a clear confirmation path.

## How to get started

Email us at [contact@backgroundready.com](mailto:contact@backgroundready.com) with which bureau you want to freeze (or both). We'll reply with next steps and scheduling.
