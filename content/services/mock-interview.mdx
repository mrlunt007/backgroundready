The **Readiness Mock Interview** focuses on how you **sound**, not just what you write on forms.

## Sample question themes

- Walk me through your last three roles and why you left.
- Explain this gap on your resume.
- What would your former manager say about your performance?
- Is there anything we should know before we run background screening?

## Outcome

You'll leave with tighter answers, fewer tangents, and phrasing that matches your written application—reducing the risk of "story drift" during verification.
