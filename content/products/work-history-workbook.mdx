The **Work History Workbook** is designed for one job: make your self-reported history match what verifiers will find.

## Who it's for

- Job seekers with multiple short-term roles
- Career changers with overlapping contract work
- Anyone who hasn't updated a master resume in years

## What you'll complete

You'll walk through each position with prompts for start/end dates, reason for leaving (private notes), and reference candidates. The workbook flags common mismatch risks—title inflation, rounded years, missing legal employer names.

## How to use it

Block 45–60 minutes. Gather old offer letters, pay stubs, or LinkedIn archives. Fill one section at a time; don't guess dates.
